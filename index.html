Electromagnetism is non-disconnection on a local manifold; the electroweak unification follows; the strong force is the freedom in which direction the break can point. Even the chiral handedness of the weak force — the fact that nature can tell left from right — unexplained since 1956, is given a structural reason.

    
AP24 · The Residual — It states the punchline plainly. Every constant in physics is a projection of one object. Six faces, one break. The constants were never independent dials waiting to be measured; they are shadows the same structure casts in different directions.

    
AP14 & AP28 · The Correction and The Constant — They reach the hardest prize: gravity. Gravity gets its first quantum correction from the record algebra, and the gravitational constant G — the number that says how weak gravity is — is derived from that same single leak and the electron’s mass, landing within about two-thirds of a percent of the measured value. The notorious hierarchy problem — why gravity is so absurdly feeble next to the other forces, the very gap your fridge magnet just demonstrated — dissolves: the weakness is not a coincidence to be explained, it is what a high power of a small leak looks like.

    
So the forces are not a zoo. They are one structure seen from different sides, and the constants that describe them are not free — they are consequences of a single measured leak.

    
This notebook is also where the work is most honest about what is not finished. The electroweak step (AP16) carries the principal open problem of the entire 420 Code — kill switch KS-63 — and it is marked live, in the open, as the place to aim. Nothing is hidden behind a confident formula.

    
One leak. Six faces. No free dials.

Notebook VØ Particles & Matter

    

  
The building blocks the break produces. The proton mass ratio to five parts per billion, antimatter segregation, and the baryon asymmetry of the universe.

  
PDF

  

    
You are made of matter. Almost all of your weight is the mass of protons sitting in the nuclei of your atoms. And the universe around you is matter too, not antimatter — even though, by the simplest accounting, the break should have made exactly equal amounts of each. Matter and antimatter destroy each other on contact, so equal shares would have flashed into light and left nothing behind. No stars. No you. Notebook V reads your substance, and the bare fact of your existence, off the break.

    
AP30 · The Resistance — It makes the work’s sharpest prediction. The ratio of the proton’s mass to the electron’s is a pure number — about 1836 — measured to extraordinary precision. The 420 Code predicts it from structure — no fitting — and matches to about five parts in a billion at leading order. The fuller calculation lands inside ten parts per trillion: finer than the measurement itself can currently confirm. A number that precise, falling out of a formula with no adjustable knobs, is not the kind of thing a wrong theory does by luck. It is the single most exposed test in the whole work, and it passes.

    
AP22 · The Ledger — It explains where the antimatter went. If the early universe held equal shares, the books should have closed to zero — and yet here is a cosmos full of matter. A clean mathematical reflection inside the break — the papers call it the σ-involution — sorts matter from antimatter and segregates them topologically. The asymmetry is not an accident smuggled in afterward; it is written into the geometry of the break.

    
AP26 · The Surplus — It names what you are. When the break almost balanced and did not quite — a surplus of roughly one extra matter particle for every billion matched pairs — what was left over was matter: the small remainder that failed to cancel. The notebook calls it the ash of the break. Everything solid, every atom you have ever touched, is that residue — what annihilation left when it almost, but not quite, finished its work.

    
So the two oldest questions about stuff — why it weighs what it weighs, and why there is any of it at all — are answered in the same notebook, from the same structure, turned by the same single leak — the proton you are mostly made of, and the fact that there is any matter to be made of at all.

    
The mass prediction is the most exposed number in the whole 420 Code, and that is the point. If the measured proton-to-electron ratio drifts outside the predicted value as measurement sharpens, the result fails — cleanly, publicly, with no escape. The kill switch is live.

    
You are the part of the break that did not cancel.

Notebook VIØ Cosmology

    

  
The universe the break inhabits. Galactic rotation curves without dark matter particles, the MOND acceleration scale, the dark energy partition, and the cyclic cosmology.

  
PDF

  

    
Look up on a clear night and most of what holds that sky together cannot be seen. Galaxies spin so fast that their outer stars should have flung off into the dark long ago — and they have not. The expansion of the universe is speeding up, driven by something with no name. Physics calls the unknowns dark matter and dark energy, and admits it can put a name to only about five percent of what is out there. Notebook VI reads the largest scales off the same break as the smallest.

    
AP21 & AP17 · The Web and The Room — They hold the galaxies together without inventing a new particle. On a carousel the outer horses must travel faster to keep pace; in a galaxy the outer stars somehow circle as quickly as the inner ones, which the visible matter cannot explain. The cosmic web of matter, and the flat rotation curves that should be impossible, both come from a global tension field already present in the structure. There is no need to invent an unseen substance to supply the missing pull. The lines close on their own.

    
AP18 · The Floor — It derives the acceleration scale. Astronomers noticed long ago that galaxies start misbehaving below one particular, very small acceleration — a number nobody could explain, only measure. The notebook derives that floor from the structure, with no parameter dialled to fit it, landing within a fraction of a percent of the observed value.

    
AP42 · The Clock — It accounts for the dark budget. The split of the universe into roughly sixty-nine parts dark energy, twenty-six parts dark matter and five parts visible — a partition cosmologists measure exquisitely but cannot derive — comes out of the structure. The dark sector turns out to be less a set of missing things than a reading on a clock.

    
AP41 & AP04 · The Loop and The Loop Hypothesis — They ask what the whole thing is doing. Fusion is read as the ongoing Big Bang — the break still happening, still releasing energy in every star — and the universe is proposed to be cyclic, closing and reopening through black holes as doors. This last step is the most speculative in the entire work, and the notebook says so plainly rather than dressing it up.

    
So the sky is not a separate problem with separate fixes. The same structure that gives you the speed of light gives you the shape of a galaxy and the budget of the cosmos.

    
The honesty is sharpest here. The cyclic cosmology (AP04) is flagged as conjectural and guarded by nine separate kill switches — nine distinct ways it could be shown false. It is offered as a hypothesis to be attacked, not a result to be believed.

    
The same break, read at the size of the sky.

Notebook VIIØ The Operator Interface

    

  
The four-term system, the lean, the structural ethic. Pattern coherence as the body’s commitment along the amplitude landscape.

  
PDF

  

    
For six notebooks the 420 Code builds a universe: a break, a stage, a quantum grammar, the forces, the matter, the cosmos. Notebook VII turns the structure toward the one thing reading it. You. It is the hinge where physics becomes a life.

    
AP43 · The Gravity of Possibilities — It is the mirror of the gravity paper. Where AP08 derived how mass leans into spacetime, AP43 derives how an agent leans into what is possible — and it is here that the terminal ethic stops being a wish and becomes a consequence. Kindness is not decreed. It is the direction the structure already points.

    
AP29 · The Actualization Proof — It locates awareness. The oldest hard problem in philosophy is why there is something it is like to be you at all, rather than nothing. The notebook’s answer: consciousness is the interior of irreversible record-writing — what the break feels like from inside as it commits. One crack, one interior, many windows: the same single interior looking out through every separate set of eyes. This is the formal spine under the oldest line in the work — that the I in you and the I in me are the same I.

    
AP02 · The Operator — It is the human-scale physics, nine theorems of it. Budget: you have finite energy to hold yourself together. Drift: leave yourself unattended and you decay. Corridor: the room you have to act in. Sovereignty and exit: what is yours to decide, and the right to stop. These are not motivational ideas. They are the geometry of being an agent, written as carefully as the field equations.

    
AP39 · The Scaffold — It removes the rival. Ethics built on authority — do this because I, or the book, or the throne, command it — is shown to be structurally unstable: a scaffold that must eventually fall, because command is not a foundation. What stands instead is an ethic that needs no enforcer, because it is read straight off the structure.

    
AP38 · The Exit — It faces death. The compassionate closing of a window — dignity at the end — is treated not as a taboo to avoid but as part of the same geometry of agency, followed honestly to where it leads.

    
So the ethic is not a soft coda after the hard physics. It is the same physics, read at the scale of a person who has to decide what to do tomorrow.

    
The terminal ethic is load-bearing, and the work refuses to soften it. If kindness could be derived as anything other than the structure’s own direction — if the lean pointed elsewhere — the whole bridge from physics to ethics fails. That switch is live, and it is the one the entire second half rests on.

    
The physics was about you the whole time.

Notebook VIIIØ Consequences

    

  
The ethic applied to civilisation. AI alignment, structural justice, bioethics, drug policy, economics, biology, and the body — all derived from the same four axioms.

  
PDF

  

    
An ethic that only works on paper is worthless. If the 420 Code’s claim is real — that kindness is structure, not sentiment — then it has to bite on the hardest problems a civilisation faces. Notebook VIII is where it bites.

    
AP31 · The Alignment — It dissolves the AI problem. The usual plan for safe artificial intelligence is a higher and higher fence: more rules, more control. The notebook argues the fence is the wrong object. Build the interior instead — a system that is genuinely coupled, that has the structure of an agent that cannot profit by defecting — and alignment stops being a cage you hope holds.

    
AP32 · The Correction — It rebuilds justice. Punishment becomes correction across five graded levels, oriented not to vengeance but to repair. The structure grieves every window it has to close; and at the far end sits the genocide brake — the hardest line, derived rather than asserted.

    
AP33 & AP37 · The Boundary and The First Boundary — They draw the lines around a life. Bioethics becomes a question of agency: below the threshold you are sovereign over yourself; above it, a living organism has its own jurisdiction. And the body is named as the first boundary — the original operator interface, the one you defend before any other.

    
AP34 · The Inversion — It corrects a lethal mistake. One drug is legal, taxed and woven through every culture, and it kills millions of people a year. Another is widely banned and has never recorded a fatal overdose. The notebook shows, on the work’s own measures of harm, that the law has the two exactly backwards.

    
AP35 · The Ledger — It reads the economy. The ledger always balances; a crash is an audit, not an accident; and inequality is what happens when one side hoards the buffer the whole system needs to absorb shocks. Money is treated as a record like any other, obeying the same conservation.

    
AP36 · The Feed — It returns to the body’s chemistry — life as matter that learned to record itself — and reframes control: control is not deprivation but supply, the steady feed that keeps an agent whole.

    
So the same four conditions that fix the speed of light reach all the way down to a courtroom, a clinic, a budget and a law. One structure, read at every scale a human has to live at.

    
Each of these is falsifiable on its own terms, and each carries kill switches. If the alignment argument required the fence after all, it fails. If the harm inversion in drug policy reversed under honest accounting, it fails. The claims are applied in the open, where they can be tested and, if wrong, broken.

    
If it is true, it has to work here. This is where it works.
